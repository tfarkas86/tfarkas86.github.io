blog init: the inblogural post

19 March 2022

Housekeeping

In the file system for my blog series, I've prefixed the file name for this blog with "001". Of course, we all know this as a best practice for ensuring files are automatically ordered, while specifying the order of magnitude for the volume of files. So I clearly expect it reasonable that I might create more than 99 blog posts, but probably no more than 999.

Is this overly ambitious? Or an expression of low self-esteem? I can't quite tell. In any case, it will surely do me for the next ... 999 weeks, so I won't have to worry about refactoring the file names for quite some time. The possibility that I will have to type a supurfluous digit every so often worries me, but it won't keep me up at night.

Additionally, Dear Reader, I must apologize for all the bad jokes so early in the game. Notice, the title of this website is FarkasTimE, which is just a little cringy. But I can't help it. My name is Tim E Farkas. My hand is forced. And "blog init"? "Inblogural post"? Oof. Sorry. Just. Sorry.

I may as well offer a little housekeeping. I don't have a strict goal for the frequency of posting here. But I would like to use this space to accomplish a few goals.

First and foremost, I need a space to help record useful technical workflows and approaches for my future self. Hopefully they may be useful for you, too, if you're a data scientist or other such math or tech nerd. Second, I really enjoy offering reviews of books or articles that I've read. I hope to include some of this here. And last, I often have thoughts about a wide variety of topics, including science, nature, software, products, statistics, math, data, ethics, and culture, and I need an outlet. A dumping ground, mostly for me, but maybe you will find some of it interesting as well. Topics like family, pets, sports, beer, home ownership, and auto and bike maintenance might weave themselves in. You have been warned.

In any case, I'm excited to have this space to share a little bit of myself, so if you've read this far, thanks for joining. I'll talk with you soon.

Tim Farkas
Albuquerque, NM
